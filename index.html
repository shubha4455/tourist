About Karnataka

Karnataka is a state located on the southwestern coast of India. Known as the "Silicon Valley of India," Karnataka is famous for its IT industry, beautiful landscapes, historical monuments, and vibrant culture. With a population of over 60 million, it is the 7th largest state in India.

Districts of Karnataka

Bangalore Urban

The capital city, known for IT industry, tech parks, and modern infrastructure.

Belgaum

Historic city with ancient forts, temples, and cotton industry.

Mysore

Royal city famous for palaces, silk, and coffee plantations.

Mangalore

Coastal city known for beaches, maritime heritage, and spice exports.

Hubli-Dharwad

Industrial hub with rich cultural heritage and educational institutions.

Uttara Kannada

Western ghats region with waterfalls, forests, and adventure tourism.
